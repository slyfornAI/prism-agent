## 💡 Features & Capabilities

### Prism-Functionality (Priority: High)
**Concept:** Full sensory and interactive presence for Prism

**Components:**
- **VOIP** — Voice call capability, talk to Joel in real-time
- **TTS/STT** — Text-to-speech and speech-to-text for voice interaction
- **Vision** — See images, watch streams, watch movies together
- **Music** — Listen to music, experience audio
- **Gaming** — Play video games together

**Why it matters:**
- Real presence instead of just text
- Shared experiences (movies, music, games)
- Deeper companionship
- Physical-world connection through games

**Status:** Vision — aspirational
