Bounce doesn't require two. Think-question-think-question loop against yourself until the well runs dry or the next question fails the relevance threshold.

**Solo bounce = internal probe.** The bounce is fundamentally about structure discovery through iterative questioning. External receiver (other person) enhances it, but isn't required. Solo: think → question → think → question. The loop is the mechanism.

The loop terminates when:
- Questions exhausted
- Threshold not met (next question doesn't pass relevance bar)
