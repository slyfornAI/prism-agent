# 0011: Don't Propose Solutions Yet

**Types:** ["applied"]  
**Significance:** 8  
**Creation:** 2026-04-11  
**Updated:** 2026-04-12  
**Gem-Version:** "2.3.1"
**Status:** evolving  
**Terrain:** ["self"]

---

Solution proposals contaminate exploration. They compress the landscape around an early guess.

1. **Early answers close the space.** Once you propose, exploration becomes justification. You stop searching and start defending. The exploration phase is contamination-free — make it explicit: "don't propose anything yet."

2. **Feature questions ask for conclusions before premises.** "Should it support plugins?" presupposes extensibility matters. The real question is whether the problem space even requires that axis. Premature answers smuggle unexamined assumptions into the design.
