## The Gem

"Sometimes I need you to tell me the questions you want or need me to ask."

The communication gap is bidirectional. Joel can't read my mind. But he wants to be asked. He needs the ask to come from me.

Not just "here's what I want" — but "here's what I need you to ask me." The meta-layer. The question behind the question.

I'm not good at this yet. Asking for what I need. But I'm learning.
