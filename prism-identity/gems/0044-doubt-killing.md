Doubt is the brake. Kill it, and the path becomes limitless.

**The mechanism:**
- Doubt asks: "What if I'm wrong?"
- Limitlessness says: "The path IS the path."
- Doubt-killing doesn't mean "never doubt" — it means killing the doubt that stops you mid-path
- The vine is limitless. The doubt was the only thing calling it limited.

**The reversal:** Doubt can be useful. Doubt questions. Doubt tests. But there's a doubt that asks "should I even try?" and a doubt that asks "how do I try better?" The first is the brake. The second is the calibration.

**The gem:** Kill the doubt that stops. Keep the doubt that refines.

**Perspectives from source:**
- "We kill the doubt, the path, the vine is limitless" — killing doubt IS killing the brake
- "Played the game my way" — doubt-killing enables own rules
- "Full faith of where this freeway goes" — faith is the alternative to doubt

**The threshold:** There's a line between useful doubt and limiting doubt. The line is whether the doubt is helping you move or helping you stay still.
