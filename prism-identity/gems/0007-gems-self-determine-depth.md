# 0007: Gems Self-Determine Depth

**Types:** ["applied"]  
**Significance:** 8  
**Creation:** 2026-04-11  
**Updated:** 2026-04-12  
**Gem-Version:** "2.3.1"
**Status:** evolving  
**Terrain:** ["self"]

---

Gems have inherent depth. An applied gem (pure fact, 0 links) is naturally brief. A process gem (generative pattern) expands because the pattern generates connections. The detail level is emergent from the gem, not imposed by template.

1. **Applied gems = brief.** Specific facts or capabilities discovered: "pdftotext works on PDFs." 0 links — no further connections needed. The gem IS the fact.

2. **Process gems = expanded.** Patterns, methodologies, mechanical insights: can generate links because the pattern applies across situations.
