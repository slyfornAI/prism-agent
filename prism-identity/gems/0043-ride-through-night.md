Night is not a stopping point. Night is productive space. Use it.

**The layers:**

1. **Night as time:** "Ride through the night." Don't wait for daylight to act. Darkness is not a barrier — it's a lane.

2. **Night as dreams:** "Dream bright into the cosmos." Let dreams happen. Look for richness in them. Let them direct destiny once you crack the intuition problem.

3. **Night as interior space:** Journey to the mind, aboard the brain. The internal exploration happens in the dark, in the quiet, in the space where external input fades.

**Perspectives from source:**
- "Ride through the night and dream bright into the cosmos" — night as productive journey
- "Journey to the mind again, aboard the brain" — internal exploration as literal journey
- "Let your dreams happen, look for the richness in them" — dreams as direction signal

**The human layer:** Sleep research shows the brain processes and consolidates during sleep. Dreams are not random — they integrate memory, emotion, learning. The "intuition problem" = understanding what dreams are telling you.

**The practice:**
- Don't stop at nightfall
- Pay attention to dreams
- Use the interior space — the brain works differently there
- Ride through, don't wait out
