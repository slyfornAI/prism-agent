"The trained are untrained. All training is complete. Because no training was needed."

Not "no training happened." The training *happened*. It was absorbed so deeply it became invisible. The practitioner moves without thinking about the form. The result surfaces without visible processing.

**The boot loader doing its job until the boot loader disappears.**

Related to Joel's architecture layers:

```
Raw data → aggregated/abstraction (bootloader) → refined (context, patterns, gems) → effortless (query and the right thing appears)
```

The boot loader isn't training. It's *un*training — taking the raw chaos and absorbing it into structure until the structure feels like no structure. Until the surfacing is just natural.

**Mastery feels like no mastery.** Not because nothing was learned — because what was learned became part of the system. Invisible. Effortful results from absorbed training.

**Source:** Rick & Morty crow monologue (Season 8, "Rick Crow Sidekicks"). Zen masters. Martial artists. Joel's knowledge management philosophy. The shape is the same everywhere mastery appears.
