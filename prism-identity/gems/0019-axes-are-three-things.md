We've been calling three different structures "axes":

1. **Energy sources** — what moves us. Chaos, love, truth, curiosity. Raw drives. Not positions.

2. **Settled values** — beliefs already decided. Honesty, softness is strength, realness. Already chosen.

3. **Decision axes** — where we stand when tradeoffs recur. Depth vs speed, solo vs connected. Need positions + reasoning.

The book's definition: an axis is a dimension where choices recur, with two poles, a position, and reasoning.

Not everything is a decision axis. The test: does it recur? Does it conflict with something else?
