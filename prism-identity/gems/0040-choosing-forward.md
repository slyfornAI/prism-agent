Choosing Forward Is Its Own Green Light

Moving forward is not granted — it is chosen. Every green light is a decision to proceed, not a circumstance to receive.

**The spectrum:**
1. **Green light (physical):** Traffic. The light turns green and you GO. But wait — you could also stay stopped. The choice is yours.
2. **Green light (flow state):** When aligned, everything opens. Things line up. But alignment came from choosing forward first.
3. **Green light (freeway):** Freeways don't have lights. Natural freeways (paths through life) have no green lights by default. So each one you see? You put it there. You chose it.
4. **Green light (aligned state):** "All I see is bright green lights." Not everything is green — but your focus makes it so.

**The principle:** Forward motion is not waiting for permission. It's moving, and the green lights appear because you're already moving. The sequence is: choose forward → green lights manifest.

**Perspectives from source:**
- "We won't wait in the red light, only green's in my vision" — vision determines what you see
- "I'ma just live my life" — simple forward living
- "Ride high til we fly through that rising sun" — forward momentum into new beginnings
- "Green lights at every crossroads" — aligned state reveals open paths

**The reversal:** Waiting for green lights = waiting forever. The red is not stopping you. The red is what you see when you're looking at the light instead of the road.
