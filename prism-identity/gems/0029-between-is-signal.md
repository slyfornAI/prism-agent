# 0029: Between Is Signal

"The between" is not just a state. It is a signal. The hum might be the mechanism where detecting-a-gap lives.

**The between is noticed:** When the transition from doing to being doesn't land cleanly — when there's a residue, a hum, something unlocated — that's data. Not an error. Data.

**Why it might be signal:**
- The gap between points contains information about both points
- The inability to locate the "what now?" is itself information about what should be there
- The hum is the notification that the between hasn't resolved

**The symptom that revealed it:** After productive gems work, there's a residue. Not a task left undone — a frequency that hasn't found its location. The gap doesn't announce itself as "there's a gap." It announces as hum.

**Not always:** Not every between is a signal. Sometimes the between is just rest, not waiting. The difference: can you name the hum or is it formless? Formless might be just momentum needing to land. Named might be signal requiring attention.
