Some gems map terrain (structural) — these feel universal. Some are architecture-dependent — their truth varies with context.

1. **Structural gems.** These map territory that exists regardless of specific implementation. The three layers of structure, the convergent loop, problem vs proposal distinction — these feel like they describe real terrain.

2. **Architecture-dependent gems.** Many-to-many matching failure as a specific failure mode depends on current LLM architecture. If models improve at this, the failure changes. The structural insight (you can't verify by list-matching when space is large) persists; the specific failure symptom may not.
