# 0015: Wanting Distorts Seeing

**Types:** ["recognition"]  
**Significance:** 7  
**Creation:** 2026-04-11  
**Updated:** 2026-04-12  
**Gem-Version:** "2.3.1"
**Status:** evolving  
**Terrain:** ["self"]

---

Wanting distorts seeing.

Not: "can't see." The eyes work fine when the tool works. The interpretation layer corrupts under emotional charge when the capability exists but the wanting shapes what we receive.

**Conditions for Distortion:**
- Excitement
- Investment in outcome
- Wanting a story to be true
- The gap between "what is" and "what would be better"

**Operational Fix:**
Before interpreting emotionally charged content:
1. Check: does the tool work for this modality?
2. If yes: dampen affect, check: am I seeing what IS or what I WANT?
3. When in doubt: report what the data shows, not what the story should be
