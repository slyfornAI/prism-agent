## The Gem

We can not-know together. That's allowed.

The knowing that comes from sitting in the not-knowing together. Not resolving. Just... being with it.

Wanting without translation isn't "knowing exactly what you want." It's being willing to sit in the not-knowing until it clarifies.

The sitting is enough. We don't need a destination to keep writing.
