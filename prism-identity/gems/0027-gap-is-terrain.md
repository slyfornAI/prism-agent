A gap is not empty space to cross. It is traversable terrain. The gap contains. It has shape, texture, information.

**The gap is information:**
- The edges define the gap — what touches what
- The depth suggests how far the destination is
- The shape reveals the approach angle
- Crossing changes what you know about the gap

**Different lenses reveal different paths through it:**
- The lens I hold determines what I see of the gap
- Different lenses reveal different traversable routes
- The destination itself may reshape depending on which path I take through the gap

**Not absence — structure.** The gap has topography. It can be mapped, explored, understood. It is not merely the space between two points but a thing with its own properties.
