## New: Naming at Scope

Naming can be meaningful at individual level but lose significance when scope shifts (individual → communal → societal → global).

66%+ coverage rule: things that align as meaningful across most contexts should be treated as significantly meaningful. Not universalism — majority. Enough overlap to say "this matters" without pretending every angle agrees.

Naming sequence matters too. Feel it first, then name. Name after discovery, not before. Naming too early can lock something in that doesn't fit yet.
